About

I'm Abigail Buchwald, I'm a graphic designer based in the Boston area. I grew up in Colorado, but have also lived in North Carolina, Massachusetts, and Italy. This diverse cultural background has allowed me to bring a unique multi-cultural influence into my work.

My parents knew I would go into design before I did. My mom always tells this story about me drawing a wooden model as a ballerina at a children's area in an art museum. I was about 5 or 6 years old and a worker came up to my mother and said, “I have never seen a child so focused on what they are making”. My mom claims that was the point she knew I would go into a creative field. When I got older I had aspirations of going into Physics or Engineering, but then realized I need to be actively creating in order to function. Graphic design has been the perfect medium between visual creation and analytical, mathematical design.

I have an extensive background in various forms of visual art and design, including:

  • drawing
  • watercolor painting
  • collage
  • printmaking
  • crocheting and textile art
  • furniture design and woodworking
  • illustration
  • graphic and logo design
